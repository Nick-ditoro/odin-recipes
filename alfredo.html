Ingredients needed to make this dish are as follows:

  • Shrimp
  • Fettuccini Noodles
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Lemon
  • Chicken Broth
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions to make this dish are as follows:

  1. Boil water over high heat.
  2. Add noodles to water and cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. Remove from heat and drain noodles.
  4. Add noodles to large pot, adding shrimp, broth, sauce, lemon, pepper and Salt.
  5. Cook over medium heat for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
