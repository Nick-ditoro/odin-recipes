Lasagna Recipe!

Lasagna can be a heavy cheese bomb. Lasagne is plural and refers to the noodles themselves, also plural. Lasagna is Italian American parlance and refers to the aforementioned cheesy composition.

Ingredients needed are as follows:

  • Flat Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • More Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Instructions are as follows:

  1. Boil water over high heat.
  2. Add noodles to water and cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. Remove from heat and drain noodles.
  4. Add noodles to pan and add cheese, meat and sauce.
  5. Cook in oven at 350 degrees F for 45 minutse.
  6. Enjoy!
